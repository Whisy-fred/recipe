Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optional filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10minutes

  • Preparation:5 minutes

  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 table spoon of butter or oil
  • optoinal fillings:cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herb

Instructions

  • Beat the egg:in a bowl, beat the egg with a pinch of salt and pepper until the are well mixed. you can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffer texture.
  • Heat the pan:Place a non stick fry pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  • Cook the omelette:Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs, tilt the pan to make sure eggs evenly coat the surface.
  • Add fillings(optional):When the egg begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your choosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  • Fold and serve:As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillingd, le it cook for another minutes, then slide it onto plate.
  • Enjoy:Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
